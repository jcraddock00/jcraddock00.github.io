Introduction

Slome

Slome is a digital publication that acts as a travel guide for tourists on the best things to do in San Luis Obispo. From the best hikes and their difficulty rating, to the best restaurants and tips from the locals about what works and what doesn’t, Slome will keep you from looking like a tourist all while enabling you to plan your time in San Luis Obispo as efficiently as possible.

So you want to go Watchin'

Whether it's movies from the bed of a truck, or it's a one-of-a-kind sunset, you'll find unmatched views and fun activities that are as visually stimulating as it gets.

How about you stay awhile?

San Luis Obispo is home to dozens of hotels located all around town that vary in price point, luxury accomoadations, and locations, including the iconic Madonna Inn.

What about your travelin'?

Due to San Luis Obispo's iconic location in the Central Coast, the 101 Freeway runs directly through town. This major freeway runs the majority of the California coast and connects with most major highways, so driving to and from SLO is a very convienent travel plan. We also have a small regional airport that flies direct to LA, SF, Denver, and Dallas, along with other major cities. Finally, we are a key stop on the AmTrak path too!

Best places to go Eatin'

San Luis Obispo is home to hundreds of locally owned restaurants. From housemade pasta and bread to famous tri-tip, you'll definitely have a fully belly after every meal.

Pair that with Drinkin'

From wineries, to breweries, to everything in between, San Luis Obispo has it all! Spend your day wine tasting in Edna Valley and finish it off with a fun night downtown, the night is always young!

How's the weather?

Want to make sure that you pack the right layers, or lack thereof? Make sure you're keeping tabs on the local weather, whether that's packing your best swimsuit, or all those Patagonia layers.

Get your booty Movin'

San Luis Obispo county has some of the most beautiful hikes in the central coast. You can have it all: A goreous view of the sunset over the ocean, the sunrise over the rolling green hills, or exploring our very own Montana de Oro state park.

How about some Explorin'

Since SLO is located in the beautiful central coast of California, there are countless things that one can do to entertain themselves while enjoying the sunshine each and every day.

Our Land's History
